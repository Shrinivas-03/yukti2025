About the Festival

YUKTI, the visionary initiative of Vice-Chancellor Dr. S. Vidyashankar, is a Regional-Level Techno Management Cultural Fest that celebrates innovation, creativity, and cultural excellence. Organized by Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) and hosted every year by its regional centers, Yukti has become a vibrant platform for students to showcase their talents, explore cutting-edge ideas, and embrace artistic expression.

VTU Kalaburagi, This year will be hosting Yukti 2025, and the excitement is bigger than ever! With the theme "Reimagining Tomorrow", Yukti 2025 is set to take place on March 10th and 11th, 2025, inviting participants to step into a future shaped by bold innovation, unbridled creativity, and endless possibilities.

Step into "Reimagining Tomorrow" — where the synergy of technology and tradition takes center stage, and your ideas soar to new heights. Yukti 2025 is the place to push boundaries, embrace creativity, and shape the future.
