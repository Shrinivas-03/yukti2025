VISVESVARAYA TECHNOLOGICAL UNIVERSITY

Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), headquartered in Belagavi, Karnataka, stands as one of India’s most prestigious technological universities. Named after Sir M. Visvesvaraya, a celebrated engineer and statesman, VTU fosters innovation, research, and academic excellence across engineering, technology, and management disciplines.

With its sprawling campus in Belagavi, VTU serves as a central hub for nurturing the next generation of technologists and leaders. The university is renowned for its robust curriculum, cutting-edge research facilities, and partnerships with industry leaders. Housing over 200 affiliated colleges, VTU empowers more than 400,000 students annually, shaping the future of engineering education.

Dedicated to pushing the boundaries of knowledge, VTU continues to inspire students to excel in a dynamic, technology-driven world.

Dr. S. Vidyashankar

Vice Chancellor, VTU, Belagavi
