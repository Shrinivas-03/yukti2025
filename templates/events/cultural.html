NRITYA - SAADHANA
(Dance- Solo & Group)

Express yourself through dance and showcase your talent in solo or group performances.

NINAAD
(Singing - Solo & Group)

Solo Singing – Semiclassical/Retro 1980’s – 1990’s

Solo Singing Rules:

  1. Each performance has a 3-minute time limit
  2. Participants will not be allowed to refer to lyrics while singing
  3. The participant will be judged on the song selection, vocal clarity, rhythm, and audience appeal
  4. Copy of lyrics should be sent to the coordinators well before the event
  5. Karaoke and musical instruments are not allowed
  6. Choice of song is open to the participants but the song should not have any slangs or derogatory language.

Pop or Rock (Rock out with your socks out):

  1. A team should have a minimum of 4 maximum of 10 members
  2. Time duration 15 mins (including set-up and clearance).
  3. The participant have to bring their own musical gear.
  4. Instruments of all kinds are welcomed.

NAVYATAA
(Ramp Walk)

Strut your style and showcase your confidence on the ramp under the theme "Be Bold For Change".

Rules & Regulations:

  1. The theme for the event will be: Be Bold For Change
  2. Single, Pair, and Group ramp walks are allowed
  3. Vulgarity is strongly prohibited. Any form of obscenity will lead to debarring the team from the contest
  4. Ramp walk will be judged on costumes, theme, walking stance, attitude, and tagline
  5. Each group should have one tagline representing their group and the theme (Be Bold For Change)
  6. The decision of the judges will be final and binding
  7. All costumes are permitted, which should maintain decency
  8. Green Room will be provided for changing purposes
  9. Participants should carry their costumes
