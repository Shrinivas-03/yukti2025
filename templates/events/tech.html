ROBO SAMARA
(Robo Race)

Design and race your manually operated robot through challenging obstacles and tracks.

Gameplay Rules:

  1. The Robot will be placed at the start line. The robot starts when the timer starts.
  2. The Robot should remain on track, otherwise, it has to start from the last checkpoint crossed.
  3. The timer will stop as soon as the robot crosses the finish line.
  4. Scoring and penalty rules will be announced on the day of the event.

Robot Specifications:

  • The machine can be wired/wireless.
  • Themachine must be controlled manually
  • Themaximumdimension of the bot should be 300mm x 300mm x 300mm (L x B x H), failing which the team will be disqualified from the competition.
  • Thetotal weight of the bot should not exceed 5 kgs.
  • Themachine must not be made from Lego parts, or any ready- made assembly kits, and it must be powered by electrically only. the use of IC engine is not allowed.
  • Therobot can be powered by a power source such as a battery fixed on the robot or by a stationary power source connected to the robot by a cord.
  • Potential between any two points on the robot should not exceed 12 v.
  • Participants should keep spare batteries, otherwise it may lead to disqualification if bot is not ready or stops in between the race.
  • Thelength of the wire (for wired bots) should be long enough, and the wire should remain slack during the complete race.
  • RACETRACK:
    • Therewill be only one start and one finish line. the track surface and course line may have unevenness.
    • Therewill be certain obstacles in the racetrack, which will try to slow down the robot.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Maximum 4 team members allowed.
  2. Only one designated robot handler per team.
  3. Other team members must remain outside game zone.
  4. Unethical behavior leads to disqualification.
  5. No interference with other robots or arena damage.
  6. Organizing committee's decisions are final.
  7. Judges' decisions are final and binding.

(Robo War)

Build a robot to push opposing robots out of the circular ring or flip them over in this exciting one-on-one knockout tournament.

Robot Specifications:

  • Maximum dimensions: 400mm x 400mm (length x breadth)
  • Maximum weight: 9-12 kg
  • No intentional splitting during matches
  • Wired/wireless operation allowed
  • Self-powered (no AC power during matches)
  • Adequate wire length for arena coverage

Gameplay Rules:

  2. 3 raids per team, 30 seconds each
  3. Teams: Alpha vs Beta

PRAGYAN
(Quiz)

Test your knowledge across diverse topics in this competitive team quiz event.

Basic Rules:

  • PRELIMINARY ROUND
    • Each team will be given a set of question papers containing 20 multiple-choice objective-type questions. The following rules will be applied for the preliminary round.
    • Time limit: 20 minutes.
    • Each question will carry 1 mark.
  • ELIMINATION ROUND:
    • Each team will be given a set of question papers containing 20 fill in the blanks type questions. The following rules will be applied for the preliminary round.
    • Time limit: 20 minutes.
    • Each question will carry 1 mark.
    • Only top four teams will be selected for final round.
    • In case of tie between two or more teams, further five questions will be asked for final selection. This step will be continued till the selection of 4 teams.
  • FINAL QUIZ/STAGE ROUND:
    The final round will be comprised of the following stages:
    Stage 1: Each round comprises of two questions for each team and the response time is 1 minute.
    • Round 1: Sports
    • Round 2: Entertainment
    • Round 3: Audio/Visual.
  • Stage 2: Each round comprises of two questions for each team and will have three lifelines phone a friend, 50:50 and flip the question. There is no time limit.
    • Round 4: Indian History
    • Round 5: Indian Geography
    • Round 6: Science and Technology
    • Round 7: Indian Constitution
    • Round 8: Current Affairs
  • Stage 3: Rapid Fire round
    The following rules will be applicable for the final round:
    • 10 marks will be awarded for the correct answer and 5 marks will be deducted for each wrong answer.
    • The response time for each question is 30 seconds and the time limit is 3 minutes.
    • There is no pass option.
    • Team discussion is allowed.
