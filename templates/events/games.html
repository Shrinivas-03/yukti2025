MISSION TALAASH
(Treasure Hunt)

"House of Secrets"

Test your wit and problem-solving skills in this exciting treasure hunt adventure!

Rules & Regulations:

  1. Each team can have a maximum of 5 members. Each team will be assigned a color code (i.e. piece of paper of their color)
  2. Team color code will be assigned with performing a task
  3. The clues are found to be in a particular order
  4. All the clues of the team are different and located at different places but the final destination, the treasure is the same. The team that finds the treasure is the Winner
  5. Each identified clue carries 10 points
  6. A team cannot skip a clue. Skipping clue leads to the Negative marking (only one chance will be given)
  7. The time given to find each clue for each time is 10 minutes only
  8. If Participants find another team's clue by any chance they are not supposed to tamper with it
  9. A violation of this rule could disqualification of the team
  10. Players of your team should not destroy property belongings if found doing so they will be disqualified and will be charged with a penalty
  11. Disqualification will be done if any team is found Malpracting
  12. Help from the volunteers, friends, and discussion among other team members leads to Negative marking and disqualification
  13. Mobile Phones are strictly banned while playing the game if found direct disqualification

BGMI
(BATTLEGROUNDS MOBILE INDIA)

Compete in intense mobile battle royale matches and prove your tactical supremacy!

Guidelines:

  1. College ID is compulsory
  2. It is a 4(+1) player team tournament. A 4 man-starter roster and up to 1 substitute.
  3. Emulators are not allowed in any game mode organized. The player will be disqualified if found using any kind of emulator.
  4. Players need to carry their mobile phones and headphone and can play on android/ios tablets/phones only.
  5. Only in-game voice chat should be used after the game is started till its completion. Bring your headphones/earphones
  6. Any use of unfair means such as aimbot, triggebot, or ESP will be disqualified
  7. Should a team/player fail to join the room in time, their squad will be disqualified
  8. The entry fee would not be refunded under any circumstances.
  9. Organizers reserve the right to accept or reject any entry without stating a reason thereoff
  10. Any player/team who attempts to disrupt matches with verbal or physical abuse in any manner will be removed from the game.
