DAKSHA
(Best Manager)

"It's hard to beat a person who never gives up."

Prepare for a thrilling journey as you dive into the ultimate managerial challenge! Showcase your grit and determination across the realms of management function.

Event Guidelines:

  1. This is an individual event
  2. All participants must be at the venue 45 minutes before the event starts
  3. There will be 4 rounds
  4. The details for each round will be explained before it begins
  5. For technical events, having a laptop is mandatory
  6. The coordinators' decisions are final
  7. The dress code is formal
  8. English will be the medium of communication
  9. Judges' decisions will be considered final and binding

Round Details:

Round 1: Aptitude Test (25 Marks)

Assessment of problem-solving, prioritization, numerical skills and general knowledge. 25 questions, 1 mark each.

Round 2: Rapid Fire Round - Psychometric Test (25 Marks)

Measures mental capabilities, behavioral styles, abilities, and personality traits. 25 questions, 1 mark each.

Round 3: Situation Analysis (25 Marks)
  • Tests critical thinking and decision-making skills
  • 60 seconds preparation time
  • 3 minutes presentation time
Round 4: Personal Interview (25 Marks)
  • Interview by panel of judges
  • CV submission required
  • Evaluation based on Resume Quality, Body Language, and Answer Justification
