DAKSH (THE BEST MANAGER)

"It's hard to beat a person who never gives up."

Test your managerial skills in this comprehensive challenge featuring real-world business scenarios.

Prepare for a thrilling journey as you dive into the Action and Excitement of the ultimate managerial challenge! Embark on an extraordinary journey, striving to elevate to the highly sought title of the best manager in the business world. Showcase your grit and determination across the realms of management function. Unleash your innate managerial instincts, strategically navigating your path to victory because this is your golden opportunity for absolute domination. What's the holdup? Seize the moment and let the conquest begin!

EVENT GUIDELINES:
  • This is an individual event.
  • All participants must be at the venue 45 minutes before the event starts.
  • There will be 4 rounds.
  • The details for each round will be explained before it begins.
  • For technical events, having a laptop is mandatory.
  • The coordinators' decisions are final, and judging criteria will be explained in advance.
  • The dress code is formal.
  • English will be the medium of communication.
  • Judges' decisions will be considered final and binding.
ROUND 1: APTITUDE TEST (25 Marks)

This round is designed to specifically assess the candidate's problem-solving, prioritization, numerical skills as well as general knowledge. This is an elimination round, consisting of 25 questions each carrying 1 mark.

ROUND 2: RAPID FIRE ROUND (PSYCHOMETRIC TEST) (25 Marks)

Psychometric tests are a standard and scientific method used to measure an individual's mental capabilities and behavioral styles. They are concerned with the objective measurement of skills and knowledge, abilities, attitudes, and personality traits like intelligence and other cognitive abilities. This is an elimination round, consisting of 25 questions each carrying 1 mark.

ROUND 3: SITUATION ANALYSIS (25 Marks)
  • This round will test the participant's critical thinking and prompt decision-making skills along with their oratory skills concerning modern business situations.
  • A situation will be provided to the participant's on-the-spot, upon which, the participants are expected to come up with a solution.
  • 60 seconds of preparation time will be allotted to each participant.
  • Each participant will be allotted a time of 3 minutes to present their analysis in front of the judges' panel.
ROUND 4: PERSONAL INTERVIEW (25 Marks)
  • A personal interview will be conducted by a panel of judges.
  • The candidate's CV needs to be submitted to the coordinator on the day of the event.
  • The candidates will be evaluated for their Quality of Resume, Body Language, and Justification of Answers.
